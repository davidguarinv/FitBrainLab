Our Focus

Exercise & Stress Resilience

Our lab investigates how physical activity can enhance stress resilience, developing evidence-based interventions to improve mental well-being.

  • Brain Resilience

    We pioneer research on exercise-based strategies to enhance stress resilience, with special focus on student populations.

  • Neurobiological Mechanisms

    Our lab investigates neurobiological mechanisms through human and animal studies to understand how physical activity protects the brain against chronic stressors.

  • Exercise Interventions

    We develop evidence-based exercise protocols that strengthen the brain's natural resilience systems.
