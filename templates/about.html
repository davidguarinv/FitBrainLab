The Fit Brain Lab Team

Meet our interdisciplinary team of researchers dedicated to understanding the connections between exercise, brain function, and mental health.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Fit Brain Lab investigates how physical activity affects brain function and mental health. Our research spans from molecular mechanisms to behavioral interventions, with the goal of developing evidence-based strategies to enhance stress resilience and mental wellbeing through exercise.

We employ a translational approach, combining animal models to understand neurobiological mechanisms with human studies to develop and test interventions. Our interdisciplinary team brings together expertise in neuroscience, psychology, exercise physiology, and data science.

Research Focus

Dr. Mul's research focuses on understanding the neurobiological mechanisms through which exercise affects brain function and stress resilience. His work spans from molecular and cellular studies to behavioral interventions, with a particular interest in translating findings from animal models to human applications.
