Try These Sample Challenges Today

These research-backed mini-challenges are taken directly from our game. Try one today and see the difference it makes!

5-Minute Study Break

For every hour of studying, take a 5-minute break to do jumping jacks, stretches, or a quick walk. Our research shows this improves information retention by 18%.

🎯 Beginner Challenge

Morning Movement

Start your day with 10 minutes of gentle movement. Try stretching, jumping jacks, or a brisk walk. This activates your brain and improves focus for your first classes.

⭐ Popular Challenge

Social Movement

Invite a friend to join you for a 20-minute walk or workout. Social exercise increases enjoyment by 26% and makes you more likely to stick with it long-term.
